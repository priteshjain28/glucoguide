Use on your phone

You're running the installable version. In Chrome, tap ⋮ menu → Add to Home screen → Install — GlucoGuide becomes a real app icon, opens full-screen, and works completely offline after the first load. All your data is stored locally on this phone (it never leaves your device). Export a CSV backup now and then, and avoid "Delete browsing data" for this site, since that also deletes locally saved app data.

GlucoGuide stores your entries so you can review them and share summaries with your care team. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, dosing recommendations, or emergency alerts.
